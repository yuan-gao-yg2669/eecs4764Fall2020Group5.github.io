Recommend System with Face Detection & App design


Our project aims to design a system for a retail store which can recognize a customer’s face and recommend products to the customer based on his/her shopping history in the store.

Contact us

Motivation

Personalized commerce has become the next frontier for retails. While product recommendation has been widely used on many e-commerce sites, we cannot see much retail stores benefiting from this technology.

An article on Forbes points out that customers spend 48% more when their shopping experience is personalized. (Jia Wertz) To bring personalized shopping experience into retail stores, we decided to design this recommendation system. With robots stepping into stores like Walmart, Lowes, and Giant Food Stores, (Blake Morgan) we can imagine a robot with our recommendation system recognizing customers, giving recommendation and even guide them to the products.

System

Our system contains four main parts: (1) Raspberry Pi face recognition module; (2) Product recommendation system; (3) Android application with voice recognition; (4) Cloud Data Storage.

Architecture

Technical Components

(1) Rasberry Pi & Facial Recognition:
First, with raspberry pi model 3B+ and its camera module, we used opencv frontface model to capture faces of customers and labeled them manually.
Then we imported collected faces to opencv recognizer to train a specific face recognition model for our customers.
In th last part, when customer is captured by camera, the model will predict a name and trasfer it to server using socket.

(2) Recommendation System:
The recommendation system can recommend product in the category that the customer is looking for. If the customer has bought products in the category, this system will compute an acceptable range of price of products in the category based on products bought by the customer and find the best-selling products in the category within the price range. However, if the customer has not purchased any products in the category, the system will recommend the best-selling product in the category without any price constraint.
We developed additional algorithms to make the recommendation system be flexible to satisfy diversified demand of customers. One can recommend best sell products under the brand that the customer is looking for or the most purchased brand of the customer. Another can recommend products that most buy with the most purchased product of the customer. Those algorithms are not applied in the current version of out project for real work, but they can be found in the code of our recommendation system for future use.

(3) Android application & Voice Recognition:
The android application is used to help the customer find the recommended product in a certain category. Utilizing the Short Sentence Recognition provided by Ali cloud, the application takes in the voice request given by the customer, containing the target category where the recommended product is about to be found. From the voice request, our application will divide the request into words and matches the words with the category database. If we found the category word in the voice request, the application will send request to the local computer, and the recommendation system will search for a suitable product and send back the product information to the application. Then the product information will be displayed on the application, containing product name, category, price, and the place to find it.

(4) Cloud Data Storage:
We take the advantage of the IoT module of Ali Cloud for cloud data storage. Our system can upload new purchase data to the cloud, use the Data Analysis parts of IoT module for data processing and download data when system needs it. By connect with Ali Cloud, every individual system used by each store can access the historical purchase record of customers and recommend products sold by each store according to each customer’s habit.
